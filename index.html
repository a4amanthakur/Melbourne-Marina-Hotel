Overview

Melbourne Marina Hotel is designed uniquely from ground up, with the objective of applying the traditional hotel with grace and elegance to the location concerned in order to embrace the relevant local culture, style and history. It is important that a stay in Melbourne Marina Hotel forms an important part of the guest’s experience of visiting Melbourne, and that is achieved through references to local architecture, artwork and ambience.
